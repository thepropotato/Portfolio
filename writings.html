Allow me to explain why I've chosen the moniker "mosagadu" for myself. In Telugu, "mosagadu" translates to "cheater". From my perspective, cinema embodies a form of deception—it fabricates reality. As a cinema enthusiast and aspiring filmmaker, I've adopted this name to symbolize my fascination with the art of storytelling and filmmaking.
